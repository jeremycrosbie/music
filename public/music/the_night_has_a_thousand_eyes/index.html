The Night Has a Thousand Eyes

March 22, 2023

I first heard this tune on Earl Klugh’s 2005 album Naked Guitar. I instantly wanted to learn this tune and figure out a way to come up with an arrangement as beautiful as Mr. Klugh. I don’t know if I accomplished that goal, but I love playing this tune and figuring out new ways of approaching it.
