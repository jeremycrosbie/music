Have You Met Miss Jones

November 14, 2019

From the musical comedy I’d Rather Be Right, Have You Met Miss Jones was composed by Lorenz Hart and Richard Rogers in 1937. This is one of my favorite tunes, in terms of the melody and at-times-tricky harmonic movement.
